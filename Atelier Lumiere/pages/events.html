Events

Upcoming Exhibitions & Events

Discover what’s unfolding at Atelier Lumière. From curated exhibitions to intimate artist conversations, each event offers a unique moment of connection through light, form, and story.

Neo-Modern: A Study in Texture

March 15 – April 30, 2025

An exploration of form, tension, and tactile memory, Neo-Modern brings together five rising contemporary artists known for transforming traditional materials into visceral, multi-sensory works. Expect canvas layered with resin, hand-bent metal frames, and sculpted installations that invite not just viewing — but presence. The gallery will be softly lit with diffused amber tones to highlight texture and shadow.



Opening Night Reception: March 15, 6–9 PM
Enjoy a selection of locally sourced hors d'oeuvres, including beet crostini, aged cheese platters, and lavender-rosemary shortbread. Sparkling wine and infused waters will be served in the courtyard lounge.

Live ambient cello music will echo gently throughout the main gallery space, enhancing the immersive tone of the evening.

Whispers in Porcelain

April 10 – May 20, 2025

Delicate, meditative, and deeply human, Whispers in Porcelain is a collection of hand-crafted ceramics from artist Elise Moreau. Each piece — from fractured bowls to hollow silhouettes — explores fragility and silence as creative forces. The exhibit is arranged across soft linen pedestals and candlelit niches, creating a contemplative flow through the gallery.


Curated Gallery Tea Experience: Saturdays, 2–5 PM
Guests are invited to enjoy a pairing of artisanal teas and small bites inspired by the exhibit’s themes — jasmine pearls, rose water madeleines, and white chocolate-dusted figs. A guided artist talk and live ceramic wheel demonstration will be held on April 20th at 3 PM.

Threads of Light

May 25 – July 10, 2025

Threads of Light is a multisensory exhibition combining textile art, suspended installations, photography, and projection mapping. Exploring themes of heritage and embodiment, the space becomes a walk-through tapestry of memory. As natural light shifts during the day, shadowplay reveals new details hidden in the woven surface



Evening Vernissage & Social: March 15, 6–9 PM

Enjoy a curated tasting menu by Maison du Miel featuring elderflower gazpacho, honey-glazed goat cheese bites, and rose-petal panna cotta. Craft cocktails and mocktails will be available, featuring botanical infusions and delicate floral garnishes. Background audio features ambient soundscapes created by Paris-based composer Claire Thérien, enhancing the exhibit’s dreamlike quality.
